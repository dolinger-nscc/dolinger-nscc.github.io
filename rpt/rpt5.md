# Projected Fatalities per Capita (Measured in per Million)

This five number chart (bar and whisker), as the previous, is a comparision at the national level for both Canada and the United States. 
This chart continues with the same logic but differs in the overall numbers presented. These numbers are static and are estimates based on the following:  

- The numbers assume the reported "political assumption" to be correct (North America is at the "peak" of the curve).
- The summary numbers on the date of the respective countries of the reported "peak" were doubled for this chart estimate.
  
  
Some outliers have been truncated from the view for sizing reasons.
