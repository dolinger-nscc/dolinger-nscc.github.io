# Fatalities per Capita (Measured in per Million)

This five number chart (bar and whisker) is a comparision at the national level for both Canada and the United States. 
The go-forward plan for this analysis is to establish metrics for percent of exposure to the virus. Utilizing the five number formula I want to attempt to use the IQR (interquartile range) and apply the 1st quartile, 3rd quartile, and the median as a measure of effectiveness of isolation and / or population density. 
Some outliers have been truncated from the view for sizing reasons. 
The numbers are updated as to the "as of" date on the graph.
