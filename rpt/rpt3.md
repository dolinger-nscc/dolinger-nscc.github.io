# Hospital Regional Cases to Fatalities Association

This plot chart shows the association between the reported cases and fatalities by each Hospital Region. The chart allows for the each of the regions to be explored within their associated province.  
As in the previous provincial chart, the numbers are summarised numbers up to the "as of" date on the graph.
