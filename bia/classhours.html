Class Hours and Expectations¶

The program is being scheduled this year, 2021-22, as a ‘blended’ delivery. The class schedule may look a little confusing at first, but this structure allows you to complete pre-requisite concepts before encountering them in a follow-up class. An example would be you will not go into Data Design II while still trying to master SQL. You will be able to apply your SQL skills from the Special Topics (SQL) course straight away.

The expectations for the course are as follows:

  • You will attend the online sessions via Teams MWF from 8:30 - 12:30 daily

  • You will attend the courses in person on Tuesdays and Thursdays at the IT Campus from 8:30 - 3:00 daily.

  • Many students find they can complete much of their work during the in-class lab times as stated above. However, they do report that projects and some extra lab time was required and required an average of about 10 - 15 hours of week of independent study.

  • The contact hours you are scheduled for amount to 21 hours. Keep this in mind if you are working part-time as well.
